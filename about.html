What is FlandersCraft all about.

FlandersCraft is a Minecraft Network which started in 2013 and to this day is known as the CastleCrafters Servers.

CastleCrafters merged with FlandersCraft due to the fact that the previous owners had no intentions on keeping the network online and therefor gave it away to a former administrator from the
CastleCrafters Servers. That same administrator from the CastleCrafters Servers was at that time the Owner of the FlandersCraft Network.
